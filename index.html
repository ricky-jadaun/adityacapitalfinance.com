Eligiblity Criteria For Business Loan

The following criteria need to be met for the smooth and successful disbursal of the loan:

Net annual income of more than Rs. 1.5 lakhs for loans up to Rs. 15 Lakhs; over Rs. 3 Lakhs for loans beyond.

Must possess relevant qualifications. Proof required for doctors, engineers, chartered accountants, and management consultants.

Profit and loss statements are necessary to meet minimum criteria.

Should have a regular credit of salary to a bank account.

Applicants must be aged between 21 and 65, with the business showing cash profits for the past two years. An audited balance sheet by a registered chartered accountant is mandatory.
